session-consolidate

Run at a natural stopping point or via the /consolidate command to harvest durable learnings from the current session into AutoMem (store, associate, update, invalidate) and to create, update, merge, deduplicate, or simplify AutoVault skills. Enumerates candidates for veto first, requires explicit per-item confirmation before any destructive skill change, and ends with a short summary of what was created, updated, and consolidated.
